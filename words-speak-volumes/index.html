February 18, 2023

Almost three months later, the time has finally come! The first presentation of 2023 took place in my high school, a place I cannot wait to leave. Words Speak Volumes featured some slight alterations, including better transitions and new illustrations.

The presentation went smoothly, but unfortunately, fewer people saw Letters and Books this time. It is not yet clear whether the project will qualify for the national round of the competition. Next time, things will be a little bit different! Words Speak Volumes will be online and broadcast on YouTube! Until next week!

December 3, 2022

Letters and Books were finally unveiled at the John Atanasoff National Autumn Tournament in Information Technology, held in Sofia, Bulgaria. Despite being the debut presentation, no serious problems ended up occurring. The laptop went into sleep mode at the end, which caused one of the video recordings to be lost forever. The phone unfortunately ended up being placed at a bad angle, which means that this presentation will not have any recordings of it posted.

With Letters and Books finishing in second place among the other participants, Words Speak Volumes is off to a good start! That, however, was the first and final presentation for this year! See you next year!
