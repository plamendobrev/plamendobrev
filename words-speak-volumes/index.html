December 3, 2022

Letters and Books were finally unveiled at the "John Atanasoff" National Autumn Tournament in Information Technology, held in Sofia, Bulgaria. Despite being the debut presentation, no serious problems ended up occurring. The laptop went to sleep in the end, which caused one of the video recordings to be lost forever. The phone unfortunately ended up being placed at a bad angle, which means that this presentation will not have any recordings of it posted.

Little criticism was received from the jury, and although this is not the main purpose of Words Speak Volumes, Letters and Books finished in second place among the other participants. Off to a good start!
